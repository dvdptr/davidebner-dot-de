Information

The sound installation »Wetware« contains gelatine, that is in motion due to the effect of sound. The sound is a low frequency composition, that constantly influences the shape which results in it splitting. The installation aims to show a performative, temporary process.

The chosen organic material refers to the human body as well as a futuristic scenario that connects the human body to technological parts. Pressure, fear and transience are components of »Wetware«.
